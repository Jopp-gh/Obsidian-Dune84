### Multi-column

```
> [!multi-column]
>
>> [!check]+ Title 1
>> 
>> - words words words  
>> - words words words  
>
>> [!info]+ Title 2
>> - words words **words**
```

> ## Title
> ![[Pict.png]]
> ## Info
> A|  B |
> ---|---|
> Text| ([Links](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Frank_Herbert)) |
