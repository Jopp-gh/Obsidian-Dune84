# Text highlights
## Inline - text colors

![txt-fat](https://github.com/Jopp-gh/Obsidian-Dune84/assets/48620536/aef8de34-c1c4-43ce-aae2-419029368c97)

`#green **Abc**`
`#blue **Abc**`
`#gray **Abc**`

![txt-ital](https://github.com/Jopp48620536/1cd27a55-eb76-4bc5-b62c-5d723e42bb39)

`#green *Abc*`
`#blue *Abc*`
`#gray *Abc*`

<br>

`#green ==text==`

>red, green, blue - highlight

## Text Lines
![help-4](https://github.com/Jopp-gh/Obsidian-Dune84/assets/48620536/d7bc068c-0ba2-4065-a2a6-247d5f29cb9e)

`Words #line-a  ==Words Words Words== Words`

`Words #line-b  ==Words Words Words== Words`

`Words #line-c  ==Words Words Words== Words` 

<br>

`#line-c` may be a quicker solution for mobile users than adding the default shortcut key double tilde, eg. `~~Words~~`

**Deutsch** `#strich-a`, `#strich-b`, `#strich-c`

<br>
